# The Caf App

The Caf App is a Progressive Web App built with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) that is revolutionizing on-campus dining for institutions of higher education.  It provides an easy-to-use frontend for schedules, ratings, point tracking, and much more.

## Getting the app at your school

Check out more info about The Caf App and contact us about having the app at your school at **[https://thecaf.app](https://thecaf.app)**
